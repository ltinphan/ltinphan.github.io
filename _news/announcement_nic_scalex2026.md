NIC Scale X is part of the "Vitalizing the Viet Nam National Innovation Center" project, funded by the Government of Japan and co-managed by UNDP and NIC, with Mitsubishi Research Institute (MRI) and Plug and Play as implementing partners. Anchored at NIC Hoa Lac, the program connects startups with investors, corporate partners, academia, and policymakers to accelerate market access and cross-border innovation collaboration between Viet Nam and Japan.
