VIPC Summit 2026 brings together nearly 1,500 delegates from global investment funds, technology corporations, policymakers, and innovative enterprises to position Vietnam as a strategic technology hub in the region. This year's event highlights key sectors including AI, robotics, semiconductors, big data, and biotechnology.

For Next Robotics, this is a valuable opportunity to connect with the investment and innovation ecosystem, share our vision for smart manufacturing and logistics automation, and gain practical insights into the trends shaping Industry 4.0 in Vietnam and across Asia.
