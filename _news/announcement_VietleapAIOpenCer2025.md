The event was to kick off the three-month VietLeap AI Accelerator program, which aims to support startups in the fields of Robotics and Artificial Intelligence in Vietnam. The program is sponsored by the National Innovation Center and JICA, supported by BCG and BambuUP, providing mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities to help these startups grow and succeed in the competitive tech landscape.
